My Hackbright Project

I chose to work on this project because I am a big fan of Spotify's app and I love live music. I wanted to create an app that you could tell your favorite artist and it would branch out through Spotify's related artists relationships and find upcoming concerts you might like in your city. One of the biggest challenges I faced while making this was speed. Searches hit both the Spotify and Songkick (for event data) APIs and took 20 seconds. After many different attempts at solving this problem, I came across the grequests library and used asynchronous requests to reduce search speed to about 4 seconds.

Technologies: Python, Flask, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy, JavaScript

APIs: Spotify, Songkick

Features: Search for concerts based on Spotify's related artist relationships. Users could save concerts to their profile, see their saved events on a map and create a playlist based on their saved events. See the code here.

Thistle Internship

I was an intern at Thistle, a healthy food and juice delivery company based in Berkeley. The project I worked on as an intern was to integrate Thistle's CRM (Intercom) with Twilio. The idea for the integration came about because customer service had to switch between two systems in order to respond to customers. Some customers were chatting with them online via Intercom and others were texting into a Google voice number for support. This meant customer service reps had to switch between the two interfaces and also that communication for each customer was being saved on two systems. With the Twilio Intercom integration, all customer conversations will be managed within Intercom. For more detail on how this was accomplished, check out this article I wrote.

Technologies: Python, Django

APIs: Intercom, Twilio
